About Catotournament
At Cato Tournament, we strive to "catch them young" and groom them for success through the fun and discipline of games and sports.
The Cato Torunament consists of multiple matches focused of discovering talented, and goal-driven youths. Each competition includes age appropriate strategies and brackets to ensure fair and concese selection.

Registration, regulations and sign-ups are easy to view on our website and social media platforms. Aspiring participants can view and select from multiple competitions, including (but not limited to) football, chess and spelling bees.

Each competition has prizes for the first, second and third place that include cash scholarships, and travel as well as consolation prizes. Opening and closing ceremonies will be conducted for each game. All tournaments will be conducted utilizing age brackets and is open to participants from 6- 16 years of age.
